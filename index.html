Multiply x Me

Unveil the Picture, One Tile at a Time!

Welcome to Multiply x Me! This game consists of 16 tiles, each containing a multiplication equation. Your goal is to solve the equations and uncover the full picture hidden behind the tiles.

How to Play

Each tile presents a multiplication equation with three possible answers. Click on the correct answer to solve the equation. If you solve it correctly, the tile will reveal a portion of the hidden picture. Continue solving the equations until you uncover the entire picture.

Game Screenshot

Background images can be chosen in the settings.

Bonus level will appear after the completion of every 3 levels.
